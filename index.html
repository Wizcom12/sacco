Smart Financial Tips

Savings Strategies

  • Start Small, Think Big:

    Begin with regular small deposits - even 5,000 UGX weekly adds up to 260,000 UGX annually!

  • Set Clear Goals:

    Whether it's education, farming, or business - having specific savings goals increases success.

  • Emergency Fund:

    Aim to save at least 3 months of expenses for unexpected situations.

Loan Management

  • Borrow Wisely:

    Only take loans for productive purposes that will generate returns.

  • Plan Repayments:

    Ensure monthly loan payments don't exceed 40% of your income.

  • Build Credit History:

    Timely repayments help you qualify for larger loans in the future.
