About Busaiga SACCO

Busaiga SACCO started in 1991 as a small ROSCA and grew into a registered financial cooperative in 2006.

Serving communities in Western Uganda, Busaiga SACCO has evolved to provide affordable savings and credit services.

Our Mission

To promote financial inclusion by offering affordable and accessible savings and credit services to the rural community, while fostering economic empowerment and self-sufficiency.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Member Empowerment
  • Inclusivity
  • Leadership

Governance

Our SACCO is governed by a board of directors elected by the members. The board ensures that the SACCO adheres to its mission, vision, and values, and that it complies with regulations.

What We Do

Savings

We offer a range of savings plans tailored to meet the needs of our members, from voluntary savings to group savings and fixed deposits.

Loans

We provide affordable loans for businesses, agriculture, education, and personal needs, helping our members achieve their financial goals.

Financial Education

Our SACCO provides financial literacy training to help members manage their finances effectively and make informed financial decisions.

Member Benefits

Secure Savings

Your savings are protected and grow with competitive interest rates.

Member Support

Community Building

Join a network of individuals working towards community empowerment and financial independence.

Access to Credit

Get loans at low interest rates to support personal and business growth.
