Our Journey

From humble beginnings as a local brewing collective to a Powerful financial cooperative in Western Uganda,

Historical Background

1991
Started with 10 founding members as a local brewing collective, led by Mr. Clovice Makune
1998
Grew to 30 members, focusing on banana-based enterprises
2003
Expanded to 150 members, transitioning to a savings-based model
2006
Officially registered as Busaiga SACCO (REG NO 6681/RCS)

Vision

A Leading SACCO providing affordable financial services to members.

Mission

To provide sustainable financial savings and credit services that will economically empower the members.

Strategic Objectives

Strengthening Credit Management System and Culture for improved portfolio quality, yield and affordability through appropriate methodology
Strengthening the SACCOs savings management systems and culture for competitiveness and savings growth as well as offering attractive return on deposit
To build customer centric system for effective and efficient Microfinance service delivery
Increase Operating Self Sufficiency ratio above the current 158%
Build an appropriate staff establishment structure to aid effective implementation of the SACCO’s strategy and nurture a performance-based system to facilitate human resource attraction, development and retention for effective implementation of Busaiga SACCO’s strategy
Computerize all departments and systems
Strengthening governance structure and nurturing an inclusive leadership in the SACCO that accountable to all stakeholders

What We Do

Savings

We offer a range of savings plans tailored to meet the needs of our members, from voluntary savings to group savings and fixed deposits.

Loans

We provide affordable loans for businesses, agriculture, education, and personal needs, helping our members achieve their financial goals.

Financial Education

Our SACCO provides financial literacy training to help members manage their finances effectively and make informed financial decisions.
